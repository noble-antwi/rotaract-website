About the club

Rooted in the birthplace of Rotary

Founded in 1992 and sponsored by the Rotary Club of Chicago — the very first Rotary club in the world — we carry that legacy forward for the next generation of service leaders.

1905 — Rotary is born

Paul Harris founds the first Rotary club in downtown Chicago.

1992 — Our club is chartered

Rotary/One sponsors the Rotaract Club of Chicago.

Today — Still serving

Socials, roundtables, and hands-on service across the city and the world.
